# AI for [Industry] Teams
## A Practical Half-Day Workshop for Business Owners and Their Teams

*Colorado AI Lab · coloradoailab.com · rob@coloradoailab.com*

### What This Is

A hands-on, no-fluff workshop that gives your team real AI skills they can use the next morning — not slides about "the future of AI," but live practice with tools that save hours each week.

Half-day (4 hours) or full day (7 hours). On-site or virtual. Your team, your pace.

---

### What We Cover

**Block 1 — Where AI Actually Fits in Your Business (45 min)**

We map your specific workflows — estimating, reporting, client communication, scheduling — and identify where AI saves the most time in your industry. You walk away with a personalized AI opportunity list, ranked by ROI.

**Block 2 — Hands-On Practice: The 3 Tools Your Team Will Actually Use (90 min)**

Not a demo. Your team works through real examples using ChatGPT, Claude, and one industry-specific tool. Each person leaves with 3 prompts they can use before lunch tomorrow.

- Drafting proposals and estimates in half the time
- Responding to client questions without starting from scratch
- Turning meeting notes into action items automatically

**Block 3 — Your First AI System (60 min)**

We build one real workflow together — email templates, a report generator, an intake form — customized to your business. This is the one thing you'll use every week.

**Block 4 — Questions, Edge Cases, What Not to Do (45 min)**

Real talk about what AI gets wrong, when not to use it, data privacy, and how to keep your team from going off the rails with it.

---

Local Colorado businesses with 10–150 employees that are:
- Spending too much time on repetitive writing tasks (proposals, emails, reports)
- Watching national competitors use AI while you wonder where to start
- Done with "AI strategy" and ready for AI that actually runs in your business

Ideal industries: construction, hospitality, professional services, real estate, healthcare admin.
